Meet Your Attorney

Monique D. Scott, LLB (Hons.)

Monique D. Scott is a passionate and experienced attorney-at-law based in Port Antonio, Portland, with over 12 years of dedicated practice in the Jamaican legal system. Called to the Bar in 2014, she holds an LLB with Honours and a legal education qualification from the Norman Manley Law School and the University of the West Indies.

Before entering private practice, Monique served as Clerk of Courts in Trelawny and St. Thomas Parish Courts, and as Crown Counsel at the Office of the Director of Public Prosecutions — experience that gives her a rare and thorough understanding of both sides of the justice system.

"To help and speak up for those who are unable to do so themselves. To ensure that Justice prevails — not only for those who can afford it, but for all who are affected by it."

— Monique D. Scott, LLB (Hons.)

Comprehensive Legal Services
Tailored to Your Needs

Criminal & Civil Litigation

Whether facing criminal charges or navigating a civil dispute, Monique provides strong, strategic representation to protect your rights and achieve the best possible outcome.

Property Management

Protecting your property interests is our priority. From lease agreements and property disputes to landlord-tenant matters, we provide expert guidance to safeguard your assets.

Family Law

Sensitive family matters deserve compassionate handling. We guide you through divorce, child custody, child support, and other family law matters with care and professionalism.

Personal Injury

If you've been injured due to someone else's negligence, you deserve compensation. We fight tirelessly to ensure you receive the justice and financial recovery you are entitled to.

Estate Administration

Managing a loved one's estate can be overwhelming. We provide clear, efficient guidance through every step of the estate administration process.

Probate Practice & Procedure

Navigating probate requires expertise and attention to detail. We handle all aspects — from filing applications to managing estate assets — so you can focus on what matters most during a difficult time.

Land Title Registration & Conveyancing

Buying or selling property in Jamaica? Our conveyancing services ensure every transfer of land, first registration, and land sale is handled correctly and securely — protecting your investment.
